OUR STORY

HAK was founded in 2018 by an 18years old Entrepreneur, HADASSAH, who loves cooking right from little.
Moving to a foreign country where LOCAL FOOD is hard to find. She decided to help cooking for Africans who can’t eat foreign food.
From there, HAK was found which is now a large African kitchen in Beijing, China.
Her stay and love in Chinese culture made her also dive into learning and cooking Chinese meals

WEIGHT LOSS STORY

After starting HADASSAH’S AFRO KITCHEN, eating a lot was an easy thing.
In a twinkle of an eye, She had gained over 20pounds in less than a year(Even though she always had a gym subscription). But we all know been persistent is hard.
At this point, Hadassah’s understood what people going through obesity were passing through. She then started her weight loss journey with KETO and INTERMITTENT FASTING plan which in two month SHE LOST OVER 25POUNDS.
Hadassah wants to use her weight loss story and journey to help and
make LOSING WEIGHT easy for people.
Hadassah is now a VEGETARIAN living an healthy life.
